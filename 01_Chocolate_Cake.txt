Chocolate Cake
-----
dry: {
    1.5 cup plain flour
    1.5 cup brown sugar
    6 Tbsp cocoa
    1.5 tsp bicarb soda
}
wet: {
    1.5 cup water
    1/2 cup oil
    3 Tbsp white vinegar
    4 tsp vanilla
}
75g chocolate, chopped

Preheat oven to 180 C
Combine dry ingredients
Add wet to dry, mix
Stir in chocolate
Bake 25-30 minutes
Poke holes with a skewer so syrup / icing sinks in
