Potato & Leek Soup (for 16)
-----
1 cup oil
4 onions, chopped
2.8 kg potatoes, chopped
8 leeks, sliced
5 litres veg stock
chives
1 can coconut cream

Fry onion & garlic until onion softens
Add potato & leek, cook until leek softens
Add stock, bring to the boil
Reduce heat, boil 20 minutes uncovered, til potato softens
Cool
Blend
Heat, add cream, stir till hot
