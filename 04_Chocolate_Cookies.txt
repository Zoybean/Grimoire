Chocolate Cookies (20)
-----
1/4 cup white sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup butter
1 no-egg (1 tsp no-egg + 2 Tbsp water)
1 tsp vanilla
1 cup self-raising flour (1 cup plain flour + 1/2 tsp baking powder)
cinnamon, nutmeg, coffee to taste
1/3 cup cocoa

Pre-heat oven to 180 C
Cream butter + sugar
Mix in egg + vanilla
Mix in dry ingredients
Make 10 minutes
