Anzac Biscuits
-----
dry: {
    1+1/4 cup flour
    1 cup oats
    1/2 cup sugar
    3/4 cup coconut
}
2 Tbsp golden syrup
150g butter
mixed: {
    1/2 tsp bicarb soda
    1+1/2 Tbsp water
}

Preheat oven to 180 C
Combine dry ingredients
Heat syrup + butter over low heat until melted
Add water + bicarb mixture to pan, remove from heat, stir
Pour wet into dry, combine thoroughly
Bake 12 minutes
