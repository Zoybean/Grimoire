Coconut Jam Drops / Macaroons (18)
-----
1 can sweetened condensed coconut milk
3 cups dessicated coconut
1 tsp vanilla
jam (if making jam drops)

Preheat oven to 180 C
Beat coconut milk with whisk
Slowly mix in coconut
Add vanilla + mix
Form into ~18 small balls
if making jam drops {
    Make depression with thumb, spoon in jam
}
Bake 20 minutes
