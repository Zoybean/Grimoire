Chocolate Chip Cookies (20)
-----
1 cup + 2 Tbsp flour
1/2 tsp bicarb soda
1/2 cup nuttelex
6 Tbsp brown sugar
5 Tbsp white sugar
1+1/2 tsp vanilla
2 Tbsp soy milk
1/2 cup chocolate chips

Preheat oven to 180 C
Cream butter + sugar
Add vanilla + milk, mix
Slowly add flour + bicarb, mixing
Fold in chocolate chips
Bake 12 minutes
