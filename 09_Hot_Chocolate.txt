Hot Chocolate
-----
1.5 litres milk
300g chopped dark chocolate
600ml coconut cream
1.5 cup cocoa powder
1 cup caster sugar

Stir sugar + 1 cup water over med-low heat until sugar dissolves
Simmer 2 minutes until thickened
Add chocolate, stir till melted
Whisk in cream, cocoa + small amount of milk
Add milk slowly til it tastes good
