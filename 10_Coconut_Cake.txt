Coconut Cake
-----
1 cup nuttelex
2 cup sugar
1 can coconut milk
2 Tbsp apple cider vinegar
1 tsp vanilla
1 tsp coconut flavour
3 cup plain flour
2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda

Preheat oven to 180 C
Cream nuttelex, sugar, coconut milk, vinegar, vanilla + coconut flavour with electric mixer for 6-7 minutes
Combine flour, baking powder + soda, mix into batter
Bake in 2 pans, 25-30 mins
Cool

Pairs well with Vanilla Frosting
