Vanilla Cupcakes
-----
1+1/4 cup flour
1/2 cup sugar
1/3 cup brown sugar
1 tsp bicarb soda
2/3 cup milk
1/3 cup oil
2 tsp cider vinegar
2 tsp vanilla

Preheat oven to 180 C
Combine dry ingredients
Separately, combine wet ingredients
Mix wet into dry
Bake 18-20 minutes until tops are slightly golden
