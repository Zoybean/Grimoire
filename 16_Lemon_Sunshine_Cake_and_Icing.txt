Lemon Sunshine Cake
-----
2 cups (275g) self-raising flour
1 cup (200g) caster sugar
1 tsp baking powder
zest of 2 lemons
juice of 1 lemon
splash of orange juice
100ml oil
170ml water

Preheat oven to 200 C
Combine dry ingredients, mix in wet
Bake for 30 minutes


Icing
-----
juice of 2 lemons
zest of 1 lemon
a bunch of icing mix
