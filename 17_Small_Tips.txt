Self Raising Flour
-----
2 tsp baking powder
1 cup (150g) plain flour


No Egg
-----
1 tsp no-egg
2 Tbsp water


Applesauce Egg
-----
1/4 cup Applesauce
1/2 tsp baking powder (optional, to lighten)


When mixing cake mix, the longer you mix, the tougher it gets (it develops the gluten, which is good for bread but not often desirable in cake.
For a fluffy cake, stop mixing when you can't see any more flour.
