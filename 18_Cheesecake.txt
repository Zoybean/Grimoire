Cheesecake
-----
Filling:
-----
3 cups raw soaked cashews
juice of 2 lemons
2/3 cup coconut oil
150ml coconut milk
1 cup maple syrup

Blend all ingredients ~ 1 min
Freeze 4-6 hours


Base:
-----
1 pack gingernut biscuits, smashed
50g butter, melted

Mix together
Press firmly into base of springform cake pan
