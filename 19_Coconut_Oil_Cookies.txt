Coconut Oil Cookies
-----
1+1/2 cup rolled oats
1 cup flour
2/3 cup sugar
1 cup coconut
1 Tbsp flaxseed
1/2 cup + 1 Tbsp coconut oil, melted
2 Tbsp maple syrup
1/2 tsp baking soda
1 Tbsp water

Preheat oven to 175 C
Mix oats, flour, sugar, coconut, flax
Melt together coconut oil + maple syrup, add baking soda
Add wet mix to dry mix
Add water, stir
Leave for 5-10 minutes to cool
Bake 15-20 minutes
Cool at least 10 mins before moving to rack
