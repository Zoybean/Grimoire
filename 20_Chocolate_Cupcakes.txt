Chocolate Cupcakes - 12
-----
1 cup soy milk
1 tsp apple cider vinegar
3/4 cup sugar
1/3 cup oil
2 tsp vanilla
1 cup plain flour
1/3 cup cocoa powder
3/4 tsp baking powder

Preheat oven to 180 C
Whisk milk and vinegar together, set aside for a few mins
Add sugar, vanilla and beat until foamy
Combine dry ingredients in a separate bowl
Add dry to wet in 2 batches, mixing thoroughly
Bake 18-20 mins
