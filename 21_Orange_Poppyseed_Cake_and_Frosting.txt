Orange Poppyseed Cake
-----
2 cups self-raising flour
1/4 cup butter
1 cup caster sugar
1 cup milk
1/4 cup orange juice
1 tsp vanilla
1/4 cup poppy seeds
1 tsp orange rind

Preheat oven to 180 C
Combine all ingredients except seeds + rind
Beat with electric beater until fluffy (~1 min)
Add seeds + rind, mix gently in
Bake 50-55 mins



Orange Buttercream Frosting
-----
3/4 cup butter
3/4 cup icing sugar
2+1/2 Tbsp orange juice
1/8 tsp vanilla
1/8 tsp orange rind

Combine all, beat till creamy
