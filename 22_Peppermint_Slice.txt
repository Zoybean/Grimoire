Peppermint Slice
-----
1+3/4 cup flour
1/3 cup cocoa
2+3/4 cup icing mixture (2 portions: 1/2 cup and 2+1/4 cup)
200g butter, melted, cooled
2 Tbsp milk
160g copha (vegetable shortening), separated in half
1+1/2 tsp peppermint essence
300g chocolate, chopped

Preheat oven to 180 C
Line 20cm x 30cm pan
Sift flour, cocoa + 1/2 cup of icing mixture together
Stir in butter
Press into pan, bake 15 mins
Cool completely in pan
Melt milk, 80g copha, 2+1/4 cup icing mixture in pan over low heat until smooth
Stir in peppermint
Pour over cooled base, refrigerate 1 hour
Melt chocolate + 80g copha in pan over low heat
Cool 10 mins, pour over mint layer
Refrigerate 3 hours

Notes:
Icing mixture is pre-mixed icing sugar + starch to prevent clumps
