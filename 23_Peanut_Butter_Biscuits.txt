Peanut Butter Biscuits - 15
-----
1/4 cup (50g) butter
5 Tbsp (120g) brown suger
5 Tbsp (200g) peanut butter
1 tsp vanilla
1+1/2 Tbsp milk
either {
    1 cup self-raising flour
} or {
    3/4 cup plain flour
    1/4 cup arrowroot
    1/2 tsp baking powder
}

Preheat oven to 180 C
Cream butter, sugar, peanut butter til smooth
Add vanilla + milk, mix well
Combine other ingredients (flour) in a separate bowl
Gradually add flour mix to wet ingredients
Form mix into balls, flatten with fork
Bake 10-15 mins, until lightly browned
