Coconut Whipped Cream
-----
1 can coconut cream
1/2 cup icing suger
1/2 tsp vanilla

Refrigerate the can overnight
Chill a large bowl for about 10 minutes
Spoon the separated cream into the bowl, leaving the liquid underneath
Beat with a mixer until creamy and smooth

Notes:
The whipped cream hardens in the refrigerator and can be kept for up to 2 weeks
